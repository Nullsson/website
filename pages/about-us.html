About Us

Passionate developers who strives to provide high quality software.

Nullsson is a growing software development studio mainly focusing on developing games and other various types of media applications. Here we exercise creativity and innovation for the love of our craft to provide the world with software we can all be proud of.

By providing both educational and entertaining content within the areas of game and media developent, we hope to be able to give our community an insight into how we work, both to teach others how professional developers work as well as learning from others how we can improve.
