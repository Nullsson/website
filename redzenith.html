About

Is a challenging arcade style jumping platformer where you have to use your hook to aid you in reaching the top. Do you have the patience and decication to beat the odds and reach the top?

Features

  • Jump up a level with the help of your built-in hook.
  • Discover 5 different areas of the game all with different mechanics.
  • Risk loosing your progress every time you make a mistake.
  • Average of 1 hour of playtime with speedrun potential.
  • High definition ambient soundtracks.
